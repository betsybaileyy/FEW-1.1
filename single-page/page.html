How Margaux and I Met

I was at my local animal shelter when I went to the "puppy yard" to see if any puppies were available for adoption. I saw the sweetest white and gold puppy and thought, wow this would be the perfect dog for me. However, I still wasn't complteley convinced. I went inside the animal shelter and asked to look at kittens. I've never particualrly been a cat person, but a kitten seemed like it might have been a better fit for my lifestyle at the time.

After spending some time with the kittens I couldn't stop thinking about the sweet puppy I had met outside. I went out again to spend some more time with her and thats when I knew, I was going to adopt her.
